Privacy Policy

Welcome to Nya Reign. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and submit your email for private launch invitations.

Information We Collect

When you submit your email via our invitation form, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Consent to receive private launch invitations and updates

We also collect non-identifiable information through cookies, such as your device type and site interactions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send private launch invitations and updates
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your consent choices
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze site performance

You can accept or decline cookies via our cookie modal on your first visit. Declining cookies may limit certain site functionalities.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent (checkbox on the invitation form)
  • Our legitimate interest in providing information about our private launch

Your Rights

You have the following rights under UK GDPR:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing and data portability requests

To exercise these rights, please contact us at nyareign@hotmail.com.

Data Retention

We retain your email and consent information only for as long as necessary to send private launch invitations and comply with legal obligations. Once you request deletion, your data will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

We use FormSubmit.co to manage email submissions. Your data is securely transmitted and stored with them in accordance with their privacy policies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at nyareign@hotmail.com.
