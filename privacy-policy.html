Privacy Policy

Welcome to Nya Reign. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you submit your email for our private launch.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Email address for private launch invitations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Notify you about our private launch and related updates.
  • Send important announcements regarding your invitation.

3. Legal Basis

By providing your email, you consent to us storing and using it for the purposes described above, in accordance with the UK GDPR.

4. Data Retention

We will store your email until the private launch is complete or until you request removal from our mailing list.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request that your email be deleted at any time.
  • Withdraw your consent for receiving updates.

To exercise these rights, contact us at nyareign@hotmail.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Third-Party Services

We use FormSubmit to process form submissions. FormSubmit handles your data securely and only sends it to our email address.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at nyareign@hotmail.com.
